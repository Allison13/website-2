Our Reason

We are two students from JCM early college high that want to spread the idea of 3D printing with people in the forms of their favorite things. We wish to provide the things that people would want, created with the process of 3D printing.

About Allison

I'm Allison,a co-creator of the 3d prints sold on the website, someone who loves 3D printing. I was introduced to the world of 3d printing during a 3d printing camp at Union University, and I thoroughly enjoyed the camp so much so that the things that I learned continued to stick with me. I've been 3d printing things for a year and I now want to share my prints with others around the world.

About Tahkale

I'm Tahkale, the co-creator of the the website. I'm someone who has come to enjoy the process and the capabities of 3D printing. I would like to share the creativity that is able to be expressed with 3D printing to people in the forms of their favorite things.
